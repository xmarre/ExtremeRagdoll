Guided Arrow v1.0.3 runtime checklist

1. Delete the previous GuidedArrow module folder before installation.
2. Camera Mode 0: verify the view points along the arrow's flight path and does not look down.
3. Camera Mode 1: verify distance and elevation remain locked while steering horizontally and vertically.
4. Verify mouse-right turns right and mouse-left turns left.
5. Toggle Show Crosshair and verify it appears only during active guidance.
6. Verify Escape/right mouse removes the crosshair and restores the player camera.
7. Verify terrain, shield and nonlethal hits restore normal mission speed automatically.
8. Verify lethal hits enter the selected kill cinematic and then return.
9. Verify RTS Camera state is restored after guidance.
10. Verify mission exit leaves no custom camera, UI layer or time-speed request active.
